Step Instructions

Up Front

Deltoids 三角肌*肩上

Dumbbell Front Raise 啞鈴前平舉

Steps:
1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, knees slightly bent, and chest up.
    Hold the dumbbells with palms facing inward towards your thighs.
2. Exhale and raise one arm forward until it is parallel to the ground.
3. Inhale as you lower your arm back to the starting position and repeat.

Push-Up 伏地挺身

Steps:
1. Ensure your body is in a neutral, upright position with your elbows at a comfortable angle of 30-45°.
    Hold the dumbbells with palms facing inward towards your thighs.
2. Inhale as you lower your body until it touches the ground.
3. Exhale as you push up until your arms are fully extended.

Bicep Muscle 二頭肌

Barbell Curl 槓鈴彎舉

Steps:
1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, holding a barbell with palms facing forward and arms hanging naturally in front of you.
2. Inhale and curl the barbell up towards your shoulders while keeping your elbows fixed at your sides.
3. Exhale as you slowly lower the barbell back to the starting position.

Reverse Grip Pull-Up 反手式引體向上

Steps:
1. Face the pull-up bar and grip it with palms facing you, hands about shoulder-width apart.
2. Inhale and pull your body up using your back and biceps until your chin is above the bar.
3. Exhale as you slowly lower yourself back down to a hanging position.

Chest Muscle 胸肌

Push-Up 伏地挺身

Steps:
1. Find a comfortable hand distance for push-ups.
2. Open your elbows to a comfortable angle of 30-45° as you lower down.
3. Inhale as you lower until your body touches the ground, then exhale as you push up to full arm extension.

Barbell Bench Press 槓鈴臥推

Steps:
1. Lie on a bench with eyes directly under the barbell, feet stable on the ground.
2. Inhale as you slowly lower the barbell towards your chest, keeping elbows at a 45-degree angle.
3. Exhale as you push the barbell back to the starting position without locking out your elbows.

Forearm 前臂

Farmer's Walk 農夫走路

Steps:
1. Stand between two dumbbells or kettlebells, feet shoulder-width apart, knees slightly bent.
2. Inhale and lift the weights while standing upright, keeping arms vertical.
3. Walk forward in a controlled manner, maintaining stability throughout.

Towel Pull-Up 毛巾引體向上

Steps:
1. Hang a sturdy towel over the pull-up bar and grip each end with palms facing each other.
2. Inhale and pull yourself up using your back and biceps until your chin is above hand level.
3. Exhale as you slowly lower yourself back down to a hanging position, maintaining muscle tension throughout the movement.

Up Back

Latissimus dors 上背肌

Barbell Shrug 槓鈴聳肩

Steps:
1. Starting Position: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, holding a barbell in front of your thighs with an overhand grip.
2. Lift Shoulders: Inhale and lift your shoulders straight up towards your ears, keeping your arms straight.
3. Control Descent: Exhale and slowly lower your shoulders back to the starting position, maintaining control throughout the movement.

Bent-Over Lateral Raise 俯身側平舉

Steps:
1. Starting Position: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart and hinge forward at the hips, keeping your back flat and knees slightly bent. Hold a dumbbell in each hand with palms facing each other.
2. Lift Dumbbells: Inhale as you lower your body until it touches the ground.
3. Control Descent: Inhale as you slowly lower the dumbbells back to the starting position, ensuring stability throughout.

Triceps Brachi 三頭肌

Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press 坐姿曲臂撐體

Steps:
1. Starting Position: Sit on a bench with back support, holding a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height with palms facing forward.
2. Press Up: Exhale and push the dumbbells overhead until your arms are fully extended without locking your elbows.
3. Control Descent: Inhale as you slowly lower the dumbbells back to shoulder height, maintaining a stable core and back.

Wall Push-Up 推牆挺身

Steps:
1. Starting Position: Stand facing a wall, about one step away. Place your hands on the wall at chest height, slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
2. Lean Towards Wall: Inhale as you bend your elbows and lean your chest towards the wall while keeping your body in a straight line.
3. Push Away from Wall: Exhale and push back to the starting position using your chest and triceps.

Infraspinatus Muscle 棘下肌

Shoulder External Rotation 肩外旋

Steps:
1. Starting Position: Stand or sit with a resistance band or light dumbbell in one hand, elbow bent at 90 degrees and pressed against your side.
2. Rotate Outward: Exhale as you rotate your forearm outward away from your body while keeping your elbow stationary.
3. Control Descent: Inhale as you return to the starting position, maintaining control throughout.

Scapular Squeeze 夾肩胛

Steps:
1. Starting Position: Stand or sit with arms at your sides.
2. Squeeze Shoulder Blades: Exhale as you squeeze your shoulder blades together, pulling them down and back without moving your arms.
3. Hold and Release: Hold for a moment before releasing and returning to the starting position.

Ｍiddle Back Muscles 中背肌

Roller Down Pull 滾輪下拉

Steps:
1. Starting Position: Kneel on the ground holding a roller or barbell with both hands extended in front of you.
2. Roll Forward: Inhale as you roll forward, extending your body while keeping the core engaged.
3. Return to Start: Exhale as you pull back to kneeling position, controlling the movement throughout.

Seated Cable Row 滑輪坐姿划船

Steps:
1. Starting Position: Sit at a cable machine with feet on the platform and knees slightly bent. Grip the handle with both hands.
2. Pull Handle Towards You: Exhale as you pull the handle towards your abdomen while leaning back slightly from hips.
3. Control Return: Inhale as you extend arms back to starting position while maintaining tension in the core.

Low Front

Obliques 側腹肌

Side Plank 側橋

Steps:
1. Start with a side-lying position with your elbows directly below your shoulders and your forearms flat on the ground.
2. Straighten your legs and lift your body so that your shoulders, hips, knees and ankles are in a straight line.
3. Tighten your abdomen, maintain this position, and control your breathing.

Russian Twists 俄羅斯轉體

Steps:
1. Sit on the ground with your knees bent, your heels slightly off the ground, your hands holding dumbbells or handball balls, and straighten your arms.
2. Rotate from the upper body, turn your arms to one side, and pay attention to the stability of your hips.
3. Control the movement back to the center and rotate to the other side.

Abdominals腹肌

Crunches 捲腹

Steps:
1. Lie down with your knees bent, feet flat, hands behind your head or chest.
2. Curl your upper body upward with the strength of your abs, and keep your shoulders off the ground with your elbows facing outward.
3. After a short pause, slowly return the upper body to its original position.

Plank 平板支撐

Steps:
1. Start with a prone position with elbows aligned with shoulders, forearms flat on the ground and toes supported by feet.
2. Tighten the core, buttocks and thighs to keep the body in a straight line.
3. Hold this position and control your breathing.

Quadriceps股四頭肌

Squats 深蹲

Steps:
1. Stand straight, feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly outward, back straight.
2. When squatting, move your hips back and bend your knees until your thighs are parallel to the ground.
3. Push the floor with your heels and bring your hips and thighs back into place.

Leg Press 腿舉

Steps:
1. Sit on a leg press, feet on the platform, knees bent.
2. Push the platform until the legs are fully straightened and then slowly put it back.
3. Control the decentralization process and don't let the platform go down quickly.

Tibialis Anterior 脛骨前肌

Toe Raises 腳踝背屈訓練

Steps:
1. Sit on a chair with your feet flat and raise one heel with your toes pointing to the ground.
2. Slowly lower it and move on to the other foot.
3. If you need to increase the intensity, you can carry out one-legged training with a load on your hand.

Backward walking 倒走

Steps:
1. Stand up straight, your heels on the ground first, and slowly start walking backwards.
2. Keep your back straight and your knees slightly bent to control your pace.
3. The time spent practicing walking is gradually increasing.

Low Back

Erector Spinae 下背肌

Deadlift 硬舉

Steps:
1. Stand, feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly outward, barbell in front of feet.
2. Hold the barbell with both hands, bend your knees slightly, straighten your back, and lift your chest.
3. Using the strength of your hip and thigh muscles, pull the barbell off the ground while keeping your back upright and standing straight. Slowly put the barbell back on the ground to make sure your back stays upright and avoids bending over.

Superman 人式

Steps:
1. Lie on the floor with your arms outstretched, your legs outstretched and your head lifted gently.
2. Lift both arms and legs at the same time, leaving the chest slightly off the ground and tightening the lower back muscles.
3. Hold for 1-2 seconds, then slowly lower back to the starting position.

Gluteus Maximus 臀大肌

Glute Bridge 臀橋

Steps:
1. Lie on your back with your knees bent, your feet flat on the floor, your arms on your sides.
2. Tighten your hips and raise them until your thighs, hips and torso are in a straight line.
3. Hold at the top for 1-2 seconds, then slowly descend back to the starting position.

Squats 深蹲

Steps:
1. Stand straight, feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly outward, back straight.
2. When squatting, move your hips back and bend your knees until your thighs are parallel to the ground.
3. Push the floor with your heels and bring your hips and thighs back into place.

Hamstrings 大腿後側肌

Romanian Deadlift 羅馬尼亞硬舉

Steps:
1. Stand, feet shoulder-width apart, hand-held barbells, knees slightly bent.
2. Push your hips back, keep your back straight, and slide the bar down your thighs below your knees until you feel a stretch on the back of your thighs.
3. Pull the body back to the starting position with the force behind the hips and thighs.

Leg Press 腿舉

Steps:
1. Sit on a leg press, feet on the platform, knees bent.
2. Push the platform until the legs are fully straightened and then slowly put it back.
3. Control the decentralization process and don't let the platform go down quickly.

Calves 腓腸肌

Standing Calf Raises 站立提踵

Steps:
1. Stand with toes pointing forward or slightly outward, feet shoulder-width apart, hands holding support.
2. Lift the heel and stand as straight as possible to allow the gastrocnemius muscle to contract completely.
3. Hold for 1-2 seconds at the highest point, then slowly lower your heel back to the starting position.

Seated Calf Raises 坐姿提腫

Steps:
1. Sit on the heel machine with your legs bent, your feet on the board of the machine, and your knees slightly bent.
2. Use the force of the sole to lift the heel and feel the gastrocnemius muscle contract completely.
3. Control the heel to slowly return to the starting position.
