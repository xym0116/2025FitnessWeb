Introduction

Welcome to our 'EZ Fitness' website.
This is a fitness teaching website that you can easily get started and live a healthier life.

Follow the steps :
1. Click on the 3D muscle map in the left column
2. View the areas you want to exercise through 3D muscle maps
3. Then select the part you want to exercise from the left column
4. Click the part
5. Choose muscles
6. Click one of exercise
7. View video and textual step-by-step instructions
